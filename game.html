MUSEUM GAME

This game is a type of walking simulator where players can explore and immerse themselves in various environments of a museum. The museum in the game exists at the boundary of reality and fantasy, featuring diverse locations such as an aquarium, a library, and a garden. In the aquarium, players can observe mysterious marine creatures, while the library offers a space for reading and contemplation. The garden allows players to enjoy the beauty of nature through a serene stroll. These elements are designed to deepen the player's experience of the game world, allowing them to engage with each location's unique sensory features.

Dress up Raina

This game is about dressing Raina appropriately for different situations. Raina will request specific outfits that match certain scenarios, and the player must choose the best clothing options to fulfill her requests. The goal of the game is to dress Raina exactly as she wants and achieve a perfect score of 100 points. The score is determined by how well the selected outfit aligns with Raina's request. If the player picks the perfect combination, they will receive the highest score. This is a casual fashion styling game where players can showcase their creativity by mixing and matching different outfits. As the game progresses, the challenges will become more difficult, requiring players to carefully consider their styling choices to meet Raina's expectations.

Choice of Destiny

This game is about the player choosing their own fate. Each choice leads to a predetermined outcome, and the player must carefully navigate through different options to find the correct path to survival. At every decision point, the player is presented with multiple choices, each leading to a unique consequence. Some choices may result in danger or failure, while others will allow the player to continue their journey. The key to success is analyzing the situation and selecting the safest or most strategic option to stay alive. This game challenges players to think critically and make the right decisions under pressure. As they progress, they will encounter increasingly complex scenarios that test their ability to foresee outcomes and choose wisely. The ultimate goal is to survive by making the best possible choices in a world where every decision matters.
